10. Annexures

10.1 Ongoing Research Projects (Institutional)

Concerned to PI of that particular project. Duration auto-calculated (same-day = 1 day).

10.2 Ongoing Research Projects (External)

Same as institutional projects with additional Funding Agency column.

10.3 Budget Utilization and Revenue Generation (in lakh rupees)

10.3.1 Budget Utilization

Concerned to CFAO. RE/BE financial year labels are auto-calculated based on the selected report year. Percentages are auto-calculated but editable if needed.

10.3.1 (A) Other Details (optional)

You can add custom budget table. You may enter additional remarks here, or upload tables and figures relevant to Budget Utilization.

10.3.2 Revenue Generation

Head-wise revenue generation. First row under "Head" is pre-filled as "Revenue generation".

10.4 Developmental Works

Concerned to Works committee, Estate section, scientists and administration. 20–40 words per developmental work with relevant figures.

10.5 Important Committees

Concerned to nodal officer or member secretary. 200–250 words per meeting with relevant figures. A table with two columns: (1) Chairman and members, (2) Names of concerned persons with designation.

10.6 Nodal Officers and Responsibilities

Concerned to CAO/AO. Default responsibilities may be edited or deleted; more rows may be added.

10.7 Distinguished Visitors

Concerned to accompanied faculties. Upload figures with captions mentioning name, designation of visitor and date of visit.

10.8 Inauguration of New Facilities

Concerned to Estate section. Tables and figures with dates, facility name in caption.

10.9 Infrastructure Development in Progress

Concerned to Estate section. Upload figures with captions containing infrastructure name.

10.10 Staff Positions / Appointments / Promotions / Transfers / New joining

Concerned to CAO/AO. Separate subsections are created under this. Under each, any comments, tables or figures may be added.

10.11 Institute in media

Concerned to I/C. Provide published paper clippings (figures) with caption as date of publication and publisher.

10.12 Other activities

Concerned to faculty. Remarks with tables and figures.
